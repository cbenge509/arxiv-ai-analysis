How to change color theme:
	go to -> tab "View" -> "Slide Master" -> click "Colors" on top -> Customize Colors

To change background:
	go to -> tab "View" -> "Slide Master" -> Right Click "Format Background" -> 
		Picture or texture fill -> chose image on "background" Folder
